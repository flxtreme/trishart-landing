# trishart — Custom Wire Flowers Landing Page

> A floral-themed landing page for a handmade wire flower business — designed to feel as personal and crafted as the products themselves, while quietly working in the background to convert browsers into buyers.

---

trishart sells something hard to capture online: handmade wire flowers, the kind of gift that means more because someone made it by hand. The challenge with a landing page like this isn't just looking nice — it's making a digital storefront feel as warm and personal as the product itself, so people trust it enough to reach out.

The whole site leans into a soft, daisy-inspired palette — dirty whites, sunshine yellow, leafy green, and earthy brown — paired with bold, confident typography that gives it a boutique, editorial feel rather than a generic template look. Every section uses asymmetric rounded corners and overlapping layouts instead of flat boxes, which keeps the page feeling handcrafted instead of mass-produced — a small design choice that mirrors the product itself.

Scrolling through the page feels alive. The hero image gently drifts as you scroll, the gallery photos shift at slightly different speeds creating subtle depth, and the background color smoothly transitions from section to section instead of snapping — small touches that make the page feel premium and considered rather than static.

But beyond the visuals, this page is built with one goal: high conversion. The gallery isn't just decoration — it's strategically placed early to build desire before asking for anything, paired with a sticky "Made For Your Moment" call-to-action that follows the visitor as they browse. Real testimonials with names and context build trust right before the final ask. And the contact form is front and center, simple, and frictionless, so there's nothing standing between "I love this" and "I want one."

It's a small business landing page doing what a great storefront window does — pulling people in with beauty, building trust with proof, and making the next step (reaching out) feel effortless. The result is a page that doesn't just look handmade — it performs like it too.
